Title: Seitan

Yield: 2 Servings

Original Page from s:

Ingredients

  • 150 g vital wheat gluten
  • 40 g plain flour
  • 1 tsp veg stock
  • 1 tsp onion powder
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • salt
  • 200 ml water

Instructions

1 Combine all ingredients (except water) in a mixing bowl, then mix in the water
2 Break into chunks and steam for 15 mins
3 Fry

Notes

Probably needs a sauce with it which it can be fried in at the end.
