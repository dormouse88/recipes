Title: Spinach, Tomato and Chickpea Curry

Rating: 3/5 stars

Ingredients

  • veg oil
  • 1 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 2 onions
  • 5 cloves garlic
  • 2 cm ginger
  • 1 can tomatoes
  • 1 can chickpeas
  • 2 tsp ground coriander seed
  • 1 tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp chilli powder
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 handfuls frozen spinach

Instructions

Serve with rice or naan

Heat oil, fry mustard seeds and cumin seeds for a minute then add the onion.

Fry for 10 mins then add the garlic and ginger and fry for a couple of minutes more.

Add tinned tomatoes and half a mug of water. Cook for 10 mins then add chickpeas, coriander, chilli powder, turmeric, salt and frozen spinach.

Cook for another 10 minutes or so then serve.
