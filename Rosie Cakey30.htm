Title: Rosie Cakey

Source: Rosie

Ingredients

  • 200 g caster sugar
  • 200 g butter
  • 4 eggs
  • 200 g self-raising flour
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 2 tbsp milk

Instructions

Mix it all together and bake 20 mins on gas mark 5.

Notes

Why does this have self-raising flour AND baking powder? I don't know.
