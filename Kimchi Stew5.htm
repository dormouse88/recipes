Title: Kimchi Stew

Ingredients

  • 1 onion
  • 1 tsp Sesame Oil
  • 1 can Kimchi
  • 1 can Mixed Beans
  • 3 Spring Onions
  • 2 tsp sugar
  • 1 tsp Salt
  • 2 tsp Gochugaru (Korean Chilli Flakes)
  • 1 tbsp Gochujang (Korean Chilli Paste)
  • 1/2 package Silken Tofu
  • 500 ml Chicken Stock

Instructions

Put the Kimchi (including brine in packet/tin) and beans in a large saucepan. Lay sliced onion on top, add salt, 2 of the spring onions, sugar, gochugaru, gochujang and stock.
Put the lid on and cook on a medium heat for 15 mins.
Mix everything together.
Lay the tofu on top of the stew and cook for another 10 mins.
Scatter the remaining spring onion on top and serve.
