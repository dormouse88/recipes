Title: Dan Dan Noodles

Rating: 2/5 stars

Source: Rosie

Ingredients

  • 3 portion noodles
  • 180 g pork mince
  • soy sauce
  • 3 tsp rice wine
  • 3 tbsp veg oil
  • peanuts
  • 2 cm ginger, minced
  • 1 tsp chilli flakes
  • 1 pack preserved chinese vegetables
  • green veg (kale, pak choi, peas)
  • spring onions
  • 3 tbsp tahini
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp black rice vinegar
  • sugar

Instructions

The Mince:
Mix the mince with 3 tsp soy sauce and rice wine to loosen. Heat the veg oil and fry the meat mix with the ginger, preserved veg, chilli flakes and peanuts until the meat is cooked.

The Sauce:
Combine the tahini and 3 tbsp water to make a runny sauce. Add 3 tbsp soy sauce and the garlic, vinegar and a pinch of sugar.

The Noodles:
Cook the noodles according to packet instructions, adding the green veg for the last minute.

Combine:
Drain the noodles, pour over the sauce and top with the mince mixture. Sprinkle with spring onions and serve.
