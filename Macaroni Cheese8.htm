Title: Macaroni Cheese

Source: Rosie

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • 1 tbsp flour
  • Milk
  • 1 tsp Mustard
  • Cheese, grated
  • Macaroni or other pasta

Instructions

Cook pasta in boiling salted water according to packet instructions. Drain, toss with a little oil to stop it sticking together and put into an oven proof dish.
Melt a blob of butter on low heat, add flour and mix to make a paste. Slowly add milk, stirring constantly until you have a smooth sauce. Add grated cheese and stir until it's melted into the sauce. Add mustard, salt and pepper to taste. You can add cream or yogurt if you've got some and a spoon of horseradish is a pretty good addition if you like the flavour.
Pour sauce over the pasta, top with grated cheese and cook under the grill until the cheese is brown and crispy.
