Title: Peanut Butter Noodles

Source: Rosie

Ingredients

  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 3 tbsp chilli oil
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 4 tbsp peanut butter
  • 3 tbsp water
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • 1 tbsp chinkiang vinegar

Instructions

Put all ingredients in a jar and shake.
Cook noodles and mix sauce in.
Optionaly top with spring onions + toasted sesame seeds.

Notes

Good with added prawns/peas/greens
