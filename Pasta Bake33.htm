Title: Pasta Bake

Yield: 4 Servings

Ingredients

  • 400 g pasta
  • 1 onion
  • 4 cloves garlic
  • 100 g cheddar
  • broccoli

Instructions

1) Cook Pasta
2) Meanwhile, fry veg (onions + garlic, then others, then passata)
3) Combine veg sauce with pasta in an oven-proof dish. Grate cheese on top. Bake or grill for a bit.

Notes

You can do a cheese sauce to make this more like a macaroni cheese dish, instead of passata.
You can put any kind of veg in really.
