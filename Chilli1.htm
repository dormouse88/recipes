Title: Chilli

Cooking time: 1 hour 10 minutes

Rating: 3/5 stars

Source: Rosie

Ingredients

  • veg oil
  • 2 onions
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 500 g mince
  • 1 can kidney beans
  • jalapenos
  • cumin
  • chilli powder
  • cayenne pepper
  • paprika
  • ground coriander seed
  • oregano
  • black pepper
  • tomato ketchup
  • 1 tsp mustard
  • 2 l stock

Instructions

Serve with jacket potatoes, rice or wraps

Fry the onion in the oil until translucent then add the garlic, mince, jalepenos and a large pinch of each of the dried spices.

Cook for a few minutes until the mince has browned then add the beans, ketchup, mustard and stock.

Simmer over a low heat for an hour or so. Check the taste. Add more salt, spices and sugar if neccesary.

Balsamic vinegar and a tsp of cocoa powder are quite good additions. It gets better with time so the longer you cook it for the better.
