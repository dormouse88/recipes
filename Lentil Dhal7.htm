Title: Lentil Dhal

Ingredients

  • 200 g Red Lentils
  • 2 cloves Garlic
  • Tomatoes
  • 4 Chillies
  • Curry Leaves
  • 1 tsp Turmeric
  • 1 tsp Salt
  • Veg Oil
  • 1 tsp Mustard Seeds
  • 1 Onion
  • 4 Chillies, Dried
  • Cheese

Instructions

Put the lentils in a saucepan and cover with a couple of cm of water.
Add whole garlic cloves, turmeric, salt, tomatoes, chillies and half a handful of curry leaves. Bring to the boil and cook on a low heat for about 20 mins, until the lentils and garlic are soft.
Mash a bit with a potato masher to break up the garlic and lentils.
Fry the mustard seeds, dried chillies and onion in veg oil until brown and crispy. This is the tarka.
Pour the tarka into the dhal while its hot and serve.
