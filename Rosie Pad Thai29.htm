Title: Rosie Pad Thai

Ingredients

  • 500 ml chicken stock
  • 500 g chicken
  • 1 tin coconut milk
  • 1 stalk lemongrass
  • 12 slices galangal
  • 5 kaffir lime leaves
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 2 tbsp fish sauce
  • 3 chillis
  • 1 lime
  • 150 g mushrooms

Instructions

1 - Simmer chicken (in small chunks) in stock for 15 mins.
2 - Add coconut milk, galangal (chopped), lemongrass (smashed and chopped), chillis, lime leaves, sugar + fish sauce. Simmer for 5 mins.
3 - Add mushrooms. Cook for another 5 mins. Add lime juice. Serve with spring onions + fresh corriander.

Notes

Adjust fish sauce, lime juice and chilli to taste.
