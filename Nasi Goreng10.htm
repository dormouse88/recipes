Title: Nasi Goreng

Rating: 4/5 stars

Ingredients

  • 300 g rice
  • veg oil
  • 1 onion
  • 4 cloves garlic
  • 3 chillies
  • 700 g sliced green veg (sprouts, kale, cabbage)
  • 2 tbsp tomato puree
  • 2 tbsp kecap manis
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp white vinegar

Instructions

You could serve a fried egg on top

Cook rice in rice cooker.

Heat the oil in a wok or large frying pan. Add the onion and fry for 5 minutes, add the garlic and chillies and cook for another couple of minutes.

Add the green veg and fry for 10 minutes or so until theyâ€™ve got some brown edges. Stir in the tomato puree,
kecap manis, salt, soy sauce and vinegar.

Cook for another 5 minutes then add the rice and fold in until well mixed.

Cook until the rice is hot, season with salt to taste and serve.
