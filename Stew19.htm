Title: Stew

Instructions

Fry some onion + garlic if using.

Put 750ml or so of stock in a big pan.

Add some ingredients such as:
spuds, carrots, beans/chickpeas, pearl barley, pasata, lentils, tomatoes, quorn pieces, frozen veg

Also add some flavour such as:
worcester sauce, soy sauce, tobasco, bay leaves, lemon, marmite, ketchup, paprika

Simmer it for an hour or so
