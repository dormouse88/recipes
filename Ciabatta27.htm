Title: Ciabatta

Yield: 2 Medium loaves

Original Page from s:

Ingredients

  • 500 g strong white bread flour
  • 1 tbsp active dried yeast

Instructions

Measure 540ml of warm water into a jug.

Mix together the flour, yeast and up to 1 tablespoon of salt in a massive bowl with a fork.

Keep mixing while very gradually adding the water. It should end up wet and sticky.

Dust a worktop with flour and knead the dough on it for 5 minutes.

Drizzle a good amount of olive oil into the bowl and put the big ball of dough in it.

Cover and leave to rise for 1 hour.

Separate the dough into two equal blobs and shape them into ciabatta.

Bake in a preheated oven at gas mark 8 for 20-25 minutes.
