Title: Okonomiyaki

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp flour
  • 1 egg
  • 5 g dashi
  • cabbage
  • bonito flakes
  • mayo
  • brown sauce
  • 1 spring onion

Instructions

Put flour and dashi in a bowl. Gradually add water while mixing to make a batter.

Mix the egg in.

Chop the cabbage and onion pretty small and mix that with the batter. Mix in any other feature ingredients.

Pour mixture into a hot, oiled frying pan and cook for about 5 minutes, then flip it and cook more.

Serve with brown sauce, mayo and bonito flakes.

Notes

Dashi not essential but try to substitute with something like soy sauce, worcestershire sauce, etc.

Type of onion not that important. Normal or red onion also fine.

Good feature ingredients:
Bacon, sausage, prawns, rice crispies, kimchi, cheese, noodles, mushrooms
