Title: Fish Pie

Yield: 4 Servings

Rating: 4/5 stars

Ingredients

  • 250 g fish, frozen
  • 1 onion
  • 1 tbsp flour
  • milk
  • 1 tsp mustard
  • 100 g peas, frozen
  • 500 g potatoes
  • butter

Instructions

Serve with boiled/steamed veg (carrots, broccoli etc).

Peel and chop potatoes into 2cm chunks. Cook in salted water for 20 mins or until soft. Mash with a bit of butter, milk, salt and pepper.

Slice the onion or leek and cook in butter on a low heat until soft.
Add flour, mix to form a paste then slowly add milk, stirring constantly until you have a thick sauce.

Add mustard, salt and pepper to taste. You can add a bit of grated cheese if you want.

Add the frozen fish, broken up into chunks and the frozen peas to the sauce.

Put the fish mix into an oven proof dish and cover with the mash.

You can put some grated cheese on top if you like.

Bake at 200c/Gas 6 for 20 mins.

Notes

Good Frozen Fish: cod, haddock, prawns etc
Using more potatoes might be an idea (maybe 1000g)
Can use a leek instead of an onion
