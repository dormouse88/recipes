Title: Pea Risotto

Yield: 4

Ingredients

  • 200 g peas
  • 1 onion
  • 4 cloves garlic
  • 750 m stock
  • 10 g basil
  • 10 g corriander leaf
  • 10 g chives
  • 250 g arborio rice
  • 1/2 lemon
  • parmesan (optional)
  • 100 g spinach (frozen)

Instructions

1 Fry onion for 3 mins
2 Add garlic and fry a little more
3 Add rice and fry for another 2 mins
4 Add the stock and simmer until it's all absorbed
5 Add peas and spinach and simmer until hot again
6 When rice is nearly ready, add herbs and lemon and simmer for another 2 mins
7 Add herbs and parmesan as garnish and serve
